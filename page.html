          Description
          This is a spacious community centre where all the food is cooked from scratch. We offer daily specials, both meat and vegetarian and a home made soup. Breakfast is served all day, along with an array of sandwiches and snacks. We try to accommodate all our customers, however small their budget is. It's a welcoming and warm space for people to sit at and work or chat. There is internet as well.
